Cloche Boutique Partner Agreement

By joining Cloche, the boutique agrees:

  • Cloche acts only as a lead generation platform.
  • Cloche does not guarantee bookings or sales.
  • Boutique Responsibilities: Maintain accurate product listings; handle customer communication, pricing, delivery, and rental terms.
  • Payment Terms: Subscription / lead-based pricing (as applicable). No guarantee of conversion from leads.
  • Platform Rights: Cloche can display boutique listings and use images for promotion.
  • Conduct: No fake listings and no misleading pricing.

"Cloche is a platform that connects users with boutiques. All services are provided directly by boutique partners."
